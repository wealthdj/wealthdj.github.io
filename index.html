The Selector

This is DJ Wealth. A decade-plus of praise on the low-end, glory on the top-line.

WealthDJ is the ongoing sonic journal of DJ Wealth — an Afrogospel selector with roots in Ilorin, Nigeria and now based in the United States, releasing mixtapes under the banner of HoWz Studios. Since cutting his first #Superstars Vol 1 in February 2014, he's turned the DJ booth into a pulpit that moves: choir vocals riding amapiano basslines, Sunday anthems stitched to afrobeats pockets, quiet-storm prayers between the drops.

Across 25+ tapes and 435,000+ streams, the work has grown into a full catalogue — Purepraises, HoWzParty, the Superstars series, AfroGospel Juice, Fearless, Church Street, Testimony, XtremGospel, the Best of Frank Edwards trilogy and more. Collaborators have included Frank Edwards, Mercy Chinwo, Tim Godfrey, Dunsin Oyekan, Tope Alabi, Sinach, Mike Abdul, Ada Ehi, Lecrae, Moses Bliss, Limoblaze, Cobhams Asuquo and scores more.

The mission is simple and devotional: make the spirit move and the feet follow.

The Journey

A decade on the decks.

2014

Superstars Vol 1 drops

The first WealthDJ tape — a debut Afrogospel mixtape featuring Tim Godfrey, Frank Edwards, Mike Abdul.

2015

The XtremGospel tape

Collaboration with Dj X-One. Frank Edwards, Freke Umoh, Jahdiel, Mike Abdul on one continuous mix.

2016

Five tapes in a year

Superstars Vol 3 & 8, Unchained (with four other DJs), and Best of Frank Edwards Part 2. A working year.

2017

Fearless & Best of Frank Edwards

Cobhams Asuquo, Dunsin Oyekan, Steve Crown, Sinach feature. Winter-Jamz closes the year.

2018

Purepraises & HoWzParty

The definitive worship tape — Mercy Chinwo, Tope Alabi, Dunsin Oyekan, Travis Greene — and the first tape branded HoWz.

2019

Church Street

A hip-hop-leaning tape with Gaise, Snatcha, Testimony Jaga, Tim Godfrey.

2020

Six-tape year · Audiomack debut

Testimony, XI, Afro-Jubilee, Flux, Best of Mike Abdul, January Slo's, Best of Frank Edwards Pt.3 — plus the move to Audiomack (Feb 2020).

2021

AfroGospel Juice & Alert Vibes

A new generation enters the mix — Moses Bliss, Maikon West, Limoblaze, Banky W, Prinx Emmanuel.

2022 — 2025

The HoWz era

Under HoWz Studios — crossing into design, photography and tech while the mixes keep shipping. 435K+ streams clocked.

2026 → 2035
