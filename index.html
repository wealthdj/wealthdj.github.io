The Selector

This is Omotola. Praise on the low-end, glory on the top-line.

WealthDJ is the ongoing sonic journal of Omotola — an Afrogospel selector building sets where the choir never really stops. Hymns get re-cut into amapiano pockets. Sunday anthems meet afrobeats rhythm. The dancefloor becomes a pew and the pew becomes a dancefloor.

Rooted in West-African worship and raised on diasporic rhythm, every mix is built to do one thing: make the spirit move and the feet follow.
